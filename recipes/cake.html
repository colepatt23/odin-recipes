This is how to make Cake

Cake

This is a picture of a birthday cake. Yours does not have to be for a birthday you can just make a regular one

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Icing

Steps:

  1. Start by mixing your flour, sugar and eggs in a bowl. Don't ask me how much do whatever you feel like. It'll be fine.
  2. After you've got a batter going all you have to do is pour it into a cake pan and throw it in the oven (aggressively) and bake for 15 minutes at 350, I think.
  3. The last step is to take the cake out of the oven, let it cool, and then decorate it however you want with the icing. Or just be boring and don't decorate it at all.
